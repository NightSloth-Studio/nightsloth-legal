Account & Data Deletion Request

Developer: NightSloth Studio
App: Time Tracker & Work Hours

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated app data, please send an email to: nightsloth.studio@gamil.com

(If you use multiple email addresses, please send the request from the email address that you use in the app.)

What to include in your email

  • The email address used for your in-app account
  • Subject line: Delete account request
  • (Optional) Any additional details that help us identify your account (e.g., username, approximate last login date)

Email template (copy & paste)

To: nightsloth.studio@gamil.com
Subject: Delete account request – Time Tracker & Work Hours

Hello NightSloth Studio,

I would like to request deletion of my account and associated data for the app "Time Tracker & Work Hours".

Account email: [YOUR ACCOUNT EMAIL]

Please confirm once the deletion has been completed.

Thank you.

Deletion timeline

We will process deletion requests within one month after receiving your email.

What data will be deleted

  • Your in-app account (e.g., account identifier)
  • Cloud-synced app data associated with your account (e.g., projects, time entries, settings) that we store and control

What data may be retained

  • Records we must keep for legal, tax, security, or fraud-prevention reasons (if applicable)
  • Purchase and billing records handled by Google Play (subscription status, transactions). These are managed by Google and are not deleted by us.
  • Third-party service providers may retain limited data according to their own policies (e.g., for security and compliance).

Note: If you have an active subscription, you may need to cancel it separately in Google Play to stop future billing.
